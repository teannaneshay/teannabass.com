The World She Builds

Strategy is the foundation. Creativity is the catalyst.

"Every brand lives or dies by the world it builds around itself. I make sure that world has a pulse."

Teanna Bass doesn't just run social accounts — she architects the cultural ecosystems that make brands matter to people who have every reason to scroll past. With seven-plus years of experience spanning global campaigns, startup launches, and creator strategy, she sits at the intersection of art direction and analytics, fandom and funnel.

She has led campaigns that generated over a billion impressions, built communities from zero to hundreds of thousands, and launched a beauty brand to profitability in its first year — all while growing a personal creator presence to nearly 50,000 followers and 5 million+ views across Instagram and TikTok.

As the founder of split matter agency and Sweet Tea Cosmetics, Teanna brings a founder's instinct to every engagement — the ability to think in systems, move fast, and never lose the thread of what makes an audience care.

She is a proud member of Delta Sigma Theta Sorority, Incorporated, an MBA holder from the illustrious Howard University's School of Business, one of 100 students chosen for Pharrell's Black Ambition HBCU program, and a believer that the best work happens when strategy and culture move as one.
